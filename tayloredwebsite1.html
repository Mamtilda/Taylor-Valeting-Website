About


Taylored Valeting & Detailing was first established on the 1st September 2014 and has grown rapidly into a successful business. Our aim is to provide a quality and tailored vehicle cleaning service designed to suit the customer's needs. Taylored Valeting & Detailing provides excellent services.


Taylored Valeting & Detailing ensures that all wheels and tyres are deep cleaned to suit customer satisfaction. This is deep cleaned with an assortment of brushes and mitts, fall out & tar removal, high pressure rinse, blown and towel dried, paint cleaner polish (on painted alloys), sealed or waxed and tyres dressed.

Taylored Valeting & Detailing also do single stage machine polish. This involves Maintenance Valet, full decontamination (tar spots, fall out and clay), machine polished with Menzerna 3 in 1 and this is finished with a gloss enhancing paint sealant.

The services also include interior deep clean. This involves Vacuumed, trim cleaned and dressed, leather cleaned and conditioned, steam stain removal, cloth seats and carpets wet vacuum, headliner cleaned and glass cleaned and polished.
